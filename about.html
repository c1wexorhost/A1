How It Works

1

Picking the Services

Scheduling convenient pickups for packages, promoting online booking, and confirming appointments for efficient service initiation.

  • Customers book pickups online, specifying date and time.
  • Same-day pickups if requested before 2 AM, otherwise next day.
  • Confirmation notification sent to customers.
2

Receiving Services

Ensuring a secure, streamlined process for accepting packages from senders, prioritizing safety, efficiency, and sender satisfaction.

  • Collect packages from senders.
  • Record and manage payments using a CRM.
  • Notify senders of successful package receipt.
3

Delivering Product

Safe and timely delivery, with express options and payment collection, prioritizing item protection.

  • Standard 1-3 day delivery, with next-day option.
  • Same-day Express delivery for urgent needs.
  • Collect COD payments from recipients upon delivery.
  • Offer various packaging options for item protection.
4

Post-Delivery Operations

Managing payments, resolving issues, engaging with customers, and improving services after delivery.

  • Deliver packages to the customer.
  • Collect and provide COD payment acknowledgment.
  • Manage feedback, address issues, and facilitate returns.
  • Drive continuous improvement and engage with customers
