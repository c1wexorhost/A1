How It Works

1

Picking the Services

Our expert delivery team takes the lead in collecting your valuable packages efficiently.

  • Customers book pickups online, specifying date and time.
  • Same-day pickups if requested before 2 AM, otherwise next day.
  • Confirmation notification sent to customers.
2

Receiving Services

Our dedicated team ensures a seamless and secure acceptance of packages from the senders, including handling Cash on Delivery (COD) payments.

  • Collect packages from senders, including COD payments
  • Record and manage payments using a CRM.
  • Notify senders of successful package receipt.
3

Delivery Services

The final leg of our journey is to deliver your packages safely and promptly into the hands of their intended recipients.

  • Standard 1-3 day delivery, with next-day option.
  • Same-day Express delivery for urgent needs.
  • Offer various packaging options for item protection.
